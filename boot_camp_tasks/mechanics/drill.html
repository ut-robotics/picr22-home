Drill over a 2.5mm hole and cut an M3 thread

  1. Get a 2.5mm drill bit. Check the drill with a caliper to make sure it is the correct size.

  2. Attach the drill bit into the chuck of the drill bench

  3. Make sure that the drill bit is centered

  4. Affix your 3D printed piece into vice chucks

  5. Place the chucks under the drill

  6. Make sure that the drill bit can go far enough to drill through the part

  7. Raise the drill bench plate if necessary

  8. Drill through the holes of the part

  9. Get a M3 tap (will be given by instructor)

  10. Affix the tap to the tap wrench

  11. Center the tap to the hole

  12. Start turning the tap into the hole clockwise

  13. Tap through the hole

  14. Bring the tap out by turning it counter-clockwise.
